Kai Fetzer


Kai currently holds the rank of Brown Belt under Josh Griffiths, Owner of Clockwork BJJ in New York City and has been training in the art for eight years. He has over a decade of experience teaching and learning in various martial arts, fitness, and wellness programs. Growing up in California and Hawaii skateboarding, surfing, windsurfing, kiteboarding, and snowboarding, has always been attracted to adrenaline based sports. While living and working in Thailand in 2007, he was training Muay Thai and came across a community of BJJ players that opened his eyes to Jiu Jitsu. He quickly became dedicated to the sport. Kai is also a certified Yoga Teacher and earned his credentials from Greenhouse Holistic in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. In addition, he also holds a red belt certification in Budokon under Cameron Shayne. He has also been certified with Circular Strength Training (CST) under Coach Scott Sonnon. Recently, Kai left his position as the Head Coach of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu at the UFC Gym in Manhattan to open up Elements Athletics in Crown Heights Brooklyn. Kai integrated all of his skills in the classes at Crown Heights newest BJJ Academy, Elements Athletics.

Certifications

  • Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Brown Belt
  • Budokon Red Belt
  • Yoga 200-hour teacher training

Mike Martin


Mike, having competed nearly half his life in his beloved sport of rugby, decided 5 years ago to have a try a Muay Thai. Having dabbled prior interest in the art, as well as several other martial arts, he and a friend began their training via multiple World Champion Thai Boxer and legend, Coban Lookchaomaesaithong. Under his tutelage and fostered by an enthusiastic passion for the "Art of 8," Mike trained competitively and assisted members as a Senior Student. This is where he met and began to train with Kai Fetzer. When Coban moved his camp in 2014, Mike graciously accepted the invitation that Ken Ng of C1 MMA extended to instruct kickboxing classes, which he applies to his coaching. Mike brings a warm knowledge of fitness, technique, and integration of healthy living to Roots Athletics.

Certifications

  • Kickboxing Instructor
  • BJJ Purple Belt
  • Capoeira White Belt
  • INN Holistic Health Certification

Daydd Snowdon-Jones


10 years ago Daf began his Budokon practice becoming a International Director/Professor/Black Belt in 2014. Budokon is a fusion of yogic, Martial & Living Arts. A 6 pillar system - movement being only 1 of the pillars. 8 years ago Daf began his Yogic journey, studying styles of Ashtanga, Anusara, Iyengar, Hatha & Katonah Method. About 5 years ago Daf began studying Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with Gui Arashiro. Among the Elements Coaching team, Daf teaches Budokon weekly. He also teaches Budokon 3-day intensive weekends in the US & Europe. He is also an Executive Head Coach in Self Expression & Leadership Programs at Landmark Worldwide in Manhattan.

Certifications

  • Budokon Black Belt
  • Landmark Executive Head Coach

Srijan Munier


Srijan is a lifelong athlete that found his love for Muay Thai during his time attending Pace University. He shifted from more ‘traditional’ sports like basketball, soccer and tennis because of the mentally and physical challenging nature of Muay Thai. He has trained for over nine years and has competed in the United States, China, and Thailand. He has been coaching and helping others in their personal Muay Thai journeys for the past four years. He firmly believes that Muay Thai is for everyone, regardless of age or fitness level. He believes training in the ultimate striking art has transformed his mind, body, and spirit and hopes to be able to share that tool with others. Srijan met coaches Kai and Mike while they all studied under world Champion Coban Lookchaomaesaithong, and shares their philosophy of training hard with a big smile.

Faye Farreles


Faye graduated from Washignton State University with a B.S. in Exercise Science. She is eager to work with people and continually learn more about the body and its potential as she helps individuals reach their goals with a little bit of hard work and focus. She cares about helping individuals of all ages live an active, healthy, and happy lifestyle. With that, she hopes to provide balance to the lives of people through Fitness. She currently holds a purple belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu under Renzo Gracie Academy and is passionate about the art. She truly believes there is something to gain for everyone in participating in Jiu Jitsu and is a firm believer that the lessons you learn on the mats will take you a long way in life. Faye is a Personal Trainer, Group Fitness Instructor, and teaches Brazilian Jiu Jitsu to all who are open to learning. She is excited to share what is most important to her with the people around her.
