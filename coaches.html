Kai Fetzer


Disciplines

Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Yoga, Budokon, Circular Strength Training

Background

Kai has been studying, teaching, living, breathing martial arts, fitness, and wellness programs since 2005. Growing up in California and Hawaii, what started with adrenaline based sports like skateboarding, surfing, windsurfing, kiteboarding, and snowboarding eventually escalated into a passion Muay Thai and then Brazilian Jiu Jitsu which he has been studying since 2007 in Thailand, and also under Josh Griffiths, Owner of Clockwork BJJ in New York City.

Coach Fetzer is also a yogi and teacher, receiving his certification from Greenhouse Holistic in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. He holds a Circular Strength Training (CST) certification under Coach Scott Sonnon, as well as extensive training in Budokon under founder and creator, Cameron Shayne.

Previously Head Coach of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu at the UFC Gym in Manhattan, Kai left his position to open up Elements Athletics in Crown Heights Brooklyn. He brings all of his skill and knowledge to his unique classes at Crown Heights’ newest BJJ Academy, Elements Athletics.

Certifications

  • Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Brown Belt
  • Budokon Red Belt
  • Yoga 200-hour teacher training

Mike Martin


Disciplines

Muay Thai

Background

Beginning with a love of rugby and competing in the sport for nearly half of his life, Mike first came to try Muay Thai in 2010 and things really took a turn when he began training with multiple World Champion Thai Boxer and legend, Coban Lookchaomaesaithong. Fostered by an enthusiastic passion for the "Art of 8," Mike began to train competitively under Coban’s tutelage and in 2014 accepted an invitation from Ken Ng of C1 MMA to instruct kickboxing classes.

Mike’s philosophy is an intersection of mixed martial arts and healthy living. As a coach, he brings a warm knowledge of fitness, technique, and healthy living to Elements Athletics.

Certifications

  • KICKBOXING INSTRUCTOR
  • BJJ PURPLE BELT
  • CAPOEIRA WHITE CORD
  • INN HOLISTIC HEALTH CERTIFICATION

Srijan Munier


Disciplines

Muay Thai

Background

Srijan is a lifelong athlete that found his love for Muay Thai during his university years when he shifted his focus from more ‘traditional’ sports like basketball, soccer, and tennis to the mental and physical challenges of Muay Thai. He has trained in Muay Thai since 2007 and has competed in the United States, China, and Thailand. He has been coaching and helping others on their personal Muay Thai journeys since 2012.

Srijan firmly believes that Muay Thai is for everyone, regardless of age or fitness level. He also believes this training has transformed his mind, body, and spirit, and he enjoys being able to share these tools with others. Srijan met fellow coaches Kai and Mike while they were all studying together under world Champion Coban Lookchaomaesaithong and shares their philosophy of training hard with a big smile.

Daydd Snowdon-Jones


Disciplines

Budokon, Yoga, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Background

Daydd, aka. Daf, began his Budokon practice in 2006 and became a International Director/Professor/Black Belt in 2014. He is dedicated to the Budokon fusion of yogic, Martial, and Living Arts—a 6 pillar system where movement is only one of the pillars. His training also includes Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with Gui Arashiro.

In 2008 Daf, began his Yogic journey, studying styles of Ashtanga, Anusara, Iyengar, Hatha, and the Katonah Method of yoga. In 2011 Daf began studying Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with Gui Arashiro. Among the Elements Coaching team, Daf teaches Budokon weekly. He also teaches Budokon 3-day intensive weekends in the US & Europe.

When he’s not at Elements, Daf is also an Executive Head Coach in Self Expression & Leadership Programs at Landmark Worldwide in Manhattan.

Certifications

  • BUDOKON BLACK BELT
  • LANDMARK EXECUTIVE HEAD COACH

KITFOX VALENTÍN / MANTEIGA


Disciplines

Capoeira

Background

Kitfox started training capoeira with Mestre Acordeon at his academy, home of The United Capoeira Association, at the age of 17. A childhood spent running through forests and scaling granite boulders over rivers in Northern California prepared him for the physical and mental rush of the capoeira roda. It only took one look at the capoeiristas flipping and kicking through the air for him to say to himself, “I’m going to learn to fly!”

Traveling over 200 miles at least twice a month to take classes, in 2001 Kitfox moved to Santa Cruz, California and joined Raízes do Brasil Capoeira under the tutelage of Mestre Papiba. Eventually Kitfox received the principled title of “Instructor”. Over the years he would explore a diverse interest in martial and movement arts, but capoeira remains his first love. Manteiga shares his love of capoeira through his teaching with an emphasis on body organization and movement.

Certifications

  • Instructor de Capoeira

JESSICA MCCARTHY


Disciplines

Yoga

Background

Jessica McCarthy is a professional dancer, yoga teacher, and Reiki Master with a deep appreciation for the connection between the mind, body, and spirit. She has been exploring movement through dance since a very early age, and at sixteen she completed all four levels of training required to become a certified Reiki Master Practitioner. Jessica communicates a passion for health and wellness is moved to guide and empower individuals to reach their highest potential.

Jessica pursued her dream of moving to New York to attend NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts, where she graduated with honors, receiving a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Dance and a minor in Psychology. She felt an innate calling to teach and during her final year of studies at NYU, she completed her training to become a 300 hour licensed Mind Body Dancer yoga teacher under the direction of TaraMarie Perri.

As a teacher, Jessica is generous and mindful. She carefully designs her classes to equip students with the tools of self-care that they can take away from the mat and into their daily lives.

Certifications

  • 300 hour Mind Body Dancer licensed yoga teacher
  • Reiki Practitioner - Level I, Level II, Level III, Master/Teacher

Hillary Lopes


Disciplines

Yoga

Background

A life built on movement--first with gymnastics, then classical ballet--yoga made its debut into Hillary's life as a therapeutic remedy to a dance related knee injury. The moment she experienced breath-based movement she traded passions and never looked back.

Over the past 6 years there have been many teachers who guided Hillary's path of uniting soul, heart, body and mind. In 2010 she attended the ISHTA 200hr training. Shortly thereafter she decided to spread the gifts she was experiencing to children and young adults by attending the Little Flower Yoga training program, training kids ages 4-18 years old in the NYC education system. In the summer of 2012 she blended her passions for community action, social change, and wellbeing by joining the Off the Mat Into the World Organization, for their leadership training in social justice. Always looking to continue learning as student herself, in the summer of 2014 Hillary was part of the inaugural vinyasa teacher training at Prema Yoga Brooklyn.

Whether Hillary is leading a small group of middle school students, older kids, or adults on the mat, she always strives toward revealing the higher self in each of us.

Certifications

  • ISHTA 200hr Teacher Training
  • Little Flower Yoga Program
  • Prema Yoga Brooklyn

PETRA LEE GHIN


Disciplines

Yoga, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Background

Petra’s journey in movement began while recovering from a shoulder injury that severely limited her mobility. Since that time she has become 400hr certified yoga teacher, and studied with Cameron Shayne, combining yoga postures and martial arts. Petra continued her yogi journey at Kula Yoga Project, deepening her knowledge of human anatomy and how the body is affected by yoga. She also furthered her yoga training with Alison West and gained a certificate in yoga and chairs, using the chair as a prop for people recovering from injuries.

Petra is a practicing martial artist who has a blue belt in Jiu jitsu, and is also a proficient kick boxer. Her goal is to encourage people from all backgrounds to find strength and flexibility through movement. She addresses the misalignment of the body through postural analysis and implements mobility exercises to help students to develop body awareness.

Certifications

  • 200 hr yoga Teacher Training, Budokon
  • 290 hr Teacher Training, Kula Yoga Project
