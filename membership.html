· Membership ·

Membership at Elements Athletics is offered on a month to month basis. Becoming a member at Elements means more than being a member at your local fitness gym. You're joining a family of like minded individuals, set on a common goal through knowledge sharing and physical exploration in movement.


We want to find members that are a good fit for us just as much as you want to find a training facility that fits your personality and needs. It's all about developing relationships and we believe that's an integral part of a positive learning process. At Elements Athletics we have no contracts that tie you to our service. If we don't satisfy your needs or continue to meet your expectations of service you are welcome to quit at any time.


Please contact us for information on special monthly rates.
