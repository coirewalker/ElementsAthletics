· Membership ·

At Elements, our membership means more than becoming a member at a regular gym. We believe that developing relationships are an integral part of the positive learning process. Here, you will be joining a family of like-minded individuals in an ego-free community with the common goal of physical exploration through movement.


We believe in being completely transparent about our membership fees. We have no sign-up or cancellation fees of any kind. Membership is offered month-to-month at $199, $165/mo with a six-month commitment, or $145/mo with a year commitment. Membership includes unlimited classes in Muay Thai, Jiu Jitsu, Budokon, and Yoga.

Budokids classes are monthly rates of $120, $155, or $180, for one, two, or three classes per week, respectively.

Personal training sessions for adults are $120 for one session (55min), and also available for package purchase at $1000 for 10 sessions (4 month expiration) and $1800 for 20 sessions (8 month expiration).

Personal training sessions for kids and teens are $100 for one session (45min), $850 for 10 sessions (4 month expiration), and $1550 for 20 sessions ( 8 month expiration).

Acroyoga (when available) at $25 per class, or $220 for 10 classes.
