· About Us ·

Physical strength. Core discipline. Mental power. Fierce dedication. Awakened humility.

Welcome to Elements Athletics, a cutting edge Mixed Movement Arts training facility in Crown Heights, Brooklyn. Our community training environment offers a cross-cultural blend of fight sports, practical self-defense, functional strength, and conditioning. We specialize in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Muay Thai, and Budokon.

Whether you’re a busy professional looking for a new fitness hobby or an experienced martial arts student committed to serious training, Elements provides an ego-free environment that will meet you where you’re at. We approach the time-honored roots of martial arts with a progressive, non-traditional mindset to create a unique, stimulating, and inclusive setting. We’re just a 3 minute walk from the Franklin Ave subway station. Come by for a visit to get a feeling for how Elements will suit your personality and lifestyle.
