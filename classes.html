Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Level 1

Proven time and time again in street fights and the cage, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is the most dominant form of self defense on the planet. Learn self defense and become prepared for realistic situations. Get an engaging workout where you will train techniques with a partner in a group class setting. Through your training, you will develop functional motor skills and strength, clerical thinking under stress and cognitive awareness. No open rolling or sparring in this intro level class. This is a Level 1 class and is highly recommended for first timers who have never done Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. These concepts will prepare you for the more advanced Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Level 2 classes.


Level 2

More advanced concepts and techniques than Level 1 class with an emphasis on practical application. This class is for students who have shown an understanding of the basic positions and movements and begin to spar or "roll" in class.

Muy Thai Kickboxing

Known also as Thai Boxing, Muay Thai is a dynamic & high-impact martial art reared over centuries in its birthplace of Thailand - and adopted and popularized in the West. Its use of punches, kicks, knees & elbows, as well as grappling, proved this art as highly effective in hand-to-hand military combat during its dawn....and deeply rooted as Thailand's national sport today. It's gained exponential notoriety in the U.S. over the past few decades and has been greatly utilized as a striking tool in MMA, due to its speed, conditioning, balance, poise & power. These attributes have been well implemented into the fitness arena, proven to shed weight, increase aerobic capacity & sculpt physiques -- now it's YOUR turn!!

Budokon

A Budokon by Cameron Shayne based flow class. Budokon is to move primal. In this body weight only movement class. Stalk like a panther. Invert, roll, walk like a croc. A combination of yoga, martial arts and meditation are combined to give you a complete body workout that leaves you feeling ZEN'd out!


BudoKIDS!

Budokids is a hybrid program that incorporates yoga, meditation and jiu jitsu. The balance of techniques keeps kids engaged while learning self defense fundamentals.
